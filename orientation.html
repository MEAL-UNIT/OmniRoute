SURAKKHA Project

Capacity Building Orientation

Six independent modules, each based on FIVDB's official policies. Complete each one's exam to earn its certificate.

Each module below is its own page with its own exam — 10 multiple-choice questions, 7 fact-check questions, and 3 short-answer reflections. You need 75% or higher on the scored questions to complete a module. Your progress is saved to your account, so you can pick up where you left off. One certificate is issued once all six modules are complete.

How scoring works

Your percentage is based on the 17 multiple-choice and fact-check questions per module. The 3 short-answer questions aren't auto-graded — a simple quiz can't fairly judge written reasoning — but they're required to submit, and are meant for your own reflection. Your progress is saved per module as you complete each one, so logging in later picks up right where you left off.
